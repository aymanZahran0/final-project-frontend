The iconic tower of gran nile tower Stands as a haven within the cosmopolitan vibrant city of cairo , characterized by the finest hospitality you can ever experience . One of Cairo most luxurious hotels, gran nile tower is directly poised on the magnificent Nile with a prime location in central Cairo , close to many cultural & tourists attractions. This stunning destination offers you the finest service & experiences, elegantly designed accommodation that includes a wide selection of room types with unique style & Egyptian warm hospitality to suit your needs, while enjoying breathtaking terrace with spectacular Nile views and Cairo skyline , a wide array of cuisines at any of our eight restaurants and lounges, including the highly acclaimed Revolving Restaurant . The 8 restaurants include a rooftop revolving restaurant. Restaurants looking through a high-ceilinged glass facade upon the Nile ,which serves international dishes . Japanese, Indian, and Italian, French cuisine to traditional Egyptian dishes in the atmospheric open-air are also available ,plus 2 bars and a cafe/pastry shop. And live entertainment is organized regularly on the hotel’s private yacht. Guests can work out in the gym or enjoy a warm hot-tub session it is also possible to relax on the waterfront terrace or to visit the hotel’s Galleria shopping and entertainment Center. The hotel offers a supervised Kid’s club offering activities for children.
