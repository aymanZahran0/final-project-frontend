Hilton Cairo Heliopolis Hotel features a Casino , and a hot tub. It has 3 swimming pools surrounded by a landscaped Garden . Wi-Fi is available in all rooms with charge and free in the lobby and restaurant . All rooms feature touches of Egyptian heritage in their decor . Each air-conditioned rooms has flat-screen Tv , a mini-bar and a work desk Upgraded rooms add balconies or patios with panoramic pool views . The casino offers the best in gaming in a fun & relaxed atmosphere. It offers the excitement of slot machines, video poker, and table games such as Blackjack and American Roulette as well as an intimate bar . Hilton Cairo Heliopolis Hotel has 18 different restaurants and snack-bars which include East Asian specialties in Lan Tania’s open kitchen & Italian cuisines at Leonardo’s . Traditional English dishes are served at Darts, the sports bar . It also consists, tennis court and a gym , as well as 23 meeting rooms .
