Jewel Zamalek Hotel provides accommodations with a garden, private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace . This 4-star hotel offers room services , 24-hour front desk and Free Wi-Fi . Certain units at the property include a balcony with a city view . The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a closet, an electric tea pot, a minibar , a safety deposit box , a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet . At jewel Zamalek Hotel every room comes with bed linen and towels. The accommodation offers a Continental breakfast. At jewel zamalek Hotel you’ll find a Restaurant serving American, intercontinental and European cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and gluten-free options can also be requested. The Hotel offers 4-star accommodation with an indoor pool.
